Introduction

Nigeria's Trusted Analytical Partner

Naturis Analytical Laboratory Services Limited is a professionally structured analytical and environmental testing laboratory established to provide high-quality, reliable, and timely laboratory services.

"To become a leading accredited analytical laboratory in Nigeria and Africa, recognized for excellence in environmental and food testing services."

Operating as the analytical and quality assurance arm of Goshen Giant Group Limited, Naturis supports internal operations while offering independent third-party testing and consultancy services to a wide range of clients.
