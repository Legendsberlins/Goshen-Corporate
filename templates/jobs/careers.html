About Goshen Giant Group

Goshen Giant Group is a diversified company with operations in food and beverage manufacturing, aquaculture & agro-allied services, logistics, and analytical laboratory services. We are committed to building sustainable systems that drive economic growth across Nigeria and West Africa.

Our Companies

Goshen Giant Foods Limited

Goshen Giant Foods Limited delivers high-quality food and beverage products that meet global standards.

Agro Global Logistics Limited

Agro Global Logistics Limited delivers reliable logistics and supply chain solutions.

Goshen Giant Integrated Farms

Goshen Giant Integrated Farms Limited delivers sustainable aquaculture, agricultural, and animal feed production solutions

Naturius Analytical Laboratory services Limited

Naturis Analytical Laboratory Services Limited delivers reliable analytical testing, quality assurance, and environmental consultancy services

Our Work Environment

At Goshen Giant Group Limited, we promote a professional, safe, and collaborative work environment driven by innovation, accountability, and continuous improvement. We uphold high health, safety, and hygiene standards, ensuring compliance with global best practices while empowering our teams to deliver quality, productivity, and sustainable growth.
